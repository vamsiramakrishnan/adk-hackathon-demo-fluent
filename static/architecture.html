Telecommunications — Use Case 4

The Proactive Network Outage
Communication Agent

An intelligent, multi-agent system that transforms network outage response from reactive chaos into proactive, high-touch customer communication — safeguarding our most valuable accounts.

The Business Problem

When an outage hits, a flood of angry support calls overwhelms our service centers before we even know what happened.

Brand reputation plummets on social media as customers discover the outage before we tell them.

Enterprise clients start the clock on costly SLA violations — every minute of silence costs real money.

Our current response is slow, manual, and fails to prioritize the customers who matter most.

C-Suite Objectives
15%
Reduce customer churn through proactive notification before they call us
30%
Lower inbound call center volume during network incidents
The Mandate

We must be the first to notify our customers, not the other way around. Minutes, not hours.

The AI Agent Solution

How the Pipeline Solves This

The instant a critical network fault is detected, the agent pipeline activates automatically. It analyzes the technical problem, identifies business impact, and orchestrates targeted communications — in minutes, not hours.

adk-fluent — The Entire Orchestration in One Expression
S.capture("alert_text") >> network_analyst >> customer_impact_analyst >> (enterprise_drafter | vip_drafter | mass_drafter) >> approval_summarizer

Trigger: Critical Network Alert

A fiber cut, routing failure, or power outage is detected by monitoring systems. The alert includes severity, affected zones, and preliminary ETR. The pipeline activates instantly.

Network Analyst
First responder — parses what broke, where, and how bad
Gemini 3.1 Pro
What it does
Immediately parses the raw network alert logs. Identifies the root cause (fiber cut, routing failure, power loss), determines the precise geographic blast radius, checks redundancy/failover status, and estimates time-to-resolution.
Why this matters
Without knowing exactly what broke, every downstream communication is guesswork. This agent converts raw telemetry into an actionable assessment in seconds — work that would take a senior NOC engineer 15-30 minutes manually.

Customer Impact Analyst
Translates technical outage into business impact
Gemini 3.1 Flash
What it does
Queries the Customer Accounts Database and cross-references the blast radius with customer locations. Stratifies the list by business priority: life-safety sites (hospitals, 911), enterprise SLA clients, government agencies, VIP residential, and general subscribers. Calculates total SLA financial exposure.
Why this matters
Not all customers are equal during an outage. A hospital losing connectivity is a life-safety emergency. An enterprise client with a $2M SLA triggers massive payouts. This prioritization is impossible to do manually across thousands of accounts in minutes.
